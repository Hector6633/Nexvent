Nexvent Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

At Nexvent, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you use our event management platform and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details such as name, email address, phone number, and billing information
  • Event-related details including bookings, preferences, and special requests
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, and usage logs

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process event bookings and manage event services
  • Communicate with you regarding inquiries, confirmations, and support
  • Improve our platform, services, and user experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Information Sharing

Nexvent does not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data only with:

  • Trusted event partners and vendors for service fulfillment
  • Payment gateways for secure transactions
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Request information about how your data is used

7. Third-Party Links

Nexvent may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@nexvent.com.
